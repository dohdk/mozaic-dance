Coalescence:

Coalescence is a showcase that we host every year during our spring semester here at James Madison University. It is a open style dance showcase that is open to all teams around the DMV area. From DC, Maryland, North Carolina, and the surrounding area. The purpose of this showcase is to highlight and have teams around the DMV area an opportunity to show the skills of their team! We aim to show and highlight as many diverse teams around us with various types of dance, music, and style. Deadline for signing up for Coalescence is December 4, 2021. If interested, email at mozaicdance@gmail.com. To see some of our performances the past years, we have included our performances from 2018-2020 below.

Events:

Not only Coalescence, we also perform in various organizations and events around the DMV. At JMU we perform for multiple organizations here such as ASU, FAM, VSA, and much more. We do our best to entertain students and faculty here at JMU with our performances. Besides JMU, we also perform at other schools around us such as VA Tech, VCU, UVA, and much more.
